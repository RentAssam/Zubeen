Biography

Zubeen Garg (born Sujit Narayan Garg on 18 November 1972) is an Indian singer, music director, actor, film director, lyricist, and poet from Assam. He is known for his work in Assamese, Bengali, Hindi, and other Indian languages.

He began his musical journey at a young age and gained recognition with his debut album "Moina Go" in 1992. Over the decades, he has become one of the most influential cultural figures in Northeast India, known for his powerful vocals and contributions to Assamese cinema and music.

Zubeen has received numerous awards including the Filmfare Awards East, Assam State Film Awards, and several national honors for his contributions to Indian music and cinema. His work often blends traditional Assamese folk elements with contemporary styles, creating a unique musical identity.

Beyond music, he is known for his humanitarian work and efforts to promote Assamese culture on national and international platforms.
